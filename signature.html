How to Setup Your Signature

1. Copy as Formatted Text: Highlight all text inside the dashed box of the example that matches your role structure. Press Ctrl + C. 2. Paste into Outlook: Open Outlook Settings > Signature. Press Ctrl + V. If a paste icon appears, choose "Keep Source Formatting." 3. Personalize: Replace "John Doe" with your name and update the Job Title, Region, and Phone Number.

Notice: The company logo and disclaimer are automatically added by the server. Do not add them manually.
