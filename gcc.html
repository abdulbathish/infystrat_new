    A Global Capability Centre (GCC) is a strategic offshore delivery center that provides specialized services, innovation capabilities, and operational support to global organizations across multiple time zones.
  • How do GCCs benefit global organizations?
    GCCs provide access to skilled talent, cost advantages, 24/7 operational support, innovation capabilities, and enhanced operational efficiency while maintaining high quality standards.

    Our GCCs offer a wide range of services including IT support, software development, business process management, research and development, analytics, and innovation services.
